Richard Sharpe

Meet Richard Sharpe a bastard son of a whore and a true British hero. Born in the gutters of London and joined the army to escape poverty. Joins the army as a private soldier and manages to do what so few have ever done by rising up the ranks to become an officer. Follow Sharpe in his adventures as he marches through India as a red coated private and through the European countries of Portugal, Span and France as a officer wearing the green jacket of the 95th rifles commanding the chosen men.
